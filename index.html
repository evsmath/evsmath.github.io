I studied Riemannian Geometry for my senior thesis under Prof. Claudio Gorodski, following the FAPESP project 21/10816-4. You can check out the thesis here.

The list of things I wish to learn more about is extensive. In general, I am deeply interested in geometrical and dynamical phenomena.
